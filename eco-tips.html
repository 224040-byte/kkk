🌱 Eco Tips

  • 💡 Turn off lights when you leave a room to save energy
  • 🚌 Choose public transport, bike, or walk instead of driving
  • 🛍️ Bring reusable bags to reduce plastic waste
  • 💧 Shorten your shower to save water
  • ♻️ Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metal whenever possible
